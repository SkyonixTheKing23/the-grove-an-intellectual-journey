# personality-test-cute

The Grove — What's Your Intellectual Personality?
An interactive personality quiz built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Answer a series of questions guided by two characters — Skyonix and Pexxelina — and discover which of twelve intellectual personalities matches the way your mind works.

What is this?
The Grove is a story-driven personality quiz inspired by pixel RPG aesthetics. Instead of a plain form, you're walked through questions by two animated pixel art characters who each represent a different side of human knowledge. At the end, you get a personalised result with a description, a trait line, and a shareable card you can send to friends.

Characters
Skyonix — guardian of life, growth, and all things felt deeply. He guides the paths of Nature, Color, Personal Development, Health, Spirituality, and Family. When Skyonix is on screen, the site shifts to its natural earthy green theme.
Pexxelina — guardian of logic, creation, and the cosmos. She guides the paths of Programming, Mathematics, Physics, Science, Philosophy, and Creative Expression. When Pexxelina appears, the entire site transitions into a soft twilight purple theme to match her energy.

Features

22 questions across three rounds, with character dialogue between each set
Animated 2-frame pixel art sprites for both characters
Dynamic theme switching — the entire site colour palette changes based on which character is active
Web Audio API sound effects — pixel blips, chimes, whooshes, and a fanfare on the result screen, all generated in-code with no audio files
Shareable result card — an animated card with floating pixels, a shimmer effect, and colour theming based on your result. Copy the link or a formatted text summary with one click
Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
No frameworks, no dependencies, no external files — pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
